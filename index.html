Works Offline

No internet?
No problem.

Hisab-Kitab works completely offline once installed. Perfect for areas with spotty internet.

1
Open app in browser
Visit hisab-kitab on Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone).
2

Android: tap ⋮ menu → "Add to Home Screen". iPhone: tap Share → "Add to Home Screen".
3
Use it like a native app
Opens full-screen with no browser bar. Works even with no internet connection.
4
Auto-sync when back online
As soon as internet returns, all your offline entries sync to the cloud automatically.

About Us

Why we built
Hisab-Kitab.

Hisab-Kitab

Nepal has over 800,000 small businesses — from tea shops in Kathmandu to hardware stores in Pokhara. Most of them run entirely on paper, WhatsApp groups, and memory. There's no affordable, Nepali-language tool designed for how they actually work.

We built Hisab-Kitab to change that. Our goal is simple: give every Nepali shopkeeper access to the same business tools that large companies have — completely free, in their own language, and simple enough to learn in under a minute.

Hisab-Kitab is built with love for Nepal's entrepreneurial spirit. Every feature is designed specifically for how Nepali businesses operate — from 13% VAT compliance to WhatsApp bill sharing to Bikram Sambat dates.
