Full Guide

How to use
Hisab-Kitab.

A complete step-by-step guide so you can use every feature to its full potential — even if you're not tech-savvy.

📱 Quick tips before you start
✅ Use Chrome on Android or Safari on iPhone for the best experience
✅ Install to your home screen so it opens like a real app
✅ Sign up once — your data syncs to all your devices automatically
✅ Your data is private — only you can see it
✅ Works in areas with bad internet — offline mode saves everything
📒Recording Daily Cash (Hisab-Kitab)
1
Open the Ledger tab
Tap the 📒 icon at the bottom of the app. This is your daily cash register.
2
Choose Cash In or Cash Out
Tap the green "Cash In" button for money received (sales). Tap red "Cash Out" for money spent (expenses, rent, salary).
3
Type the description and amount
Example: Description = "Rice Sale", Amount = 500. If you have stock items, type the item name and a dropdown will suggest it automatically with the price filled in.
4
Select category and tap Add
Pick Sales, Purchase, Rent, Salary etc. Then tap "+ Add Entry". Done! The entry appears instantly.
When you sell a stock item by name, the stock count automatically decreases — no manual update needed!
🧾Creating & Sending Invoices
1
Open the Invoice tab
Tap the 🧾 icon. Tap "+ Create New Invoice".
2
Fill in the details
Your business name and PAN number auto-fill from your profile. Enter the buyer's name and the date.
3
Add line items
Type each item, quantity, and rate. VAT (13%) is calculated automatically. The total updates live.
4
Save and share
Tap "Save & PDF" to download a professional PDF. Tap "WhatsApp" to send the bill directly to the customer.
Tap any saved invoice to mark it as Paid. All invoices are saved in your history forever.
📦Managing Your Stock / Inventory
1
Go to the Stock tab
Tap the 📦 icon. Add each item you sell or keep in stock.
2
Add an item
Enter the item name, quantity you have, unit (kg/pcs/litre), cost price, and selling price. Set a "Low Alert" number — you'll be warned when stock drops below it.
3
Update stock manually if needed
Tap "Edit" next to any item. Enter +10 to add 10 units or -5 to remove 5 units. Select the reason (purchase/sale/damage).
4
Watch for low-stock warnings
Items below your alert level show a red ⚠ badge. The AI advisor will also remind you to reorder.
Travel agent or consultant? Go to Settings → Business Mode → Services to switch the Stock tab to a Services catalogue instead.
📊Reading Your Reports
1
Open the Report tab
Tap 📊. You'll see your income vs expense for the last 7 days as a bar chart.
2
Check expense categories
The bar chart below shows which category costs you the most. If Rent is 60% of expenses, that's a signal to review it.
3
View monthly tax summary
Scroll down to see each month's total sales, expenses, profit, and VAT collected. Hand this to your accountant.
4
Export or share
Go to Ledger → tap the PDF button for a printable report, or WhatsApp to share your daily summary.
The AI tab gives you instant plain-English (or Nepali) analysis of your reports. Just ask "Am I profitable this month?"
🤝Setting Up Partner / Shared Accounts
1
Owner creates the workspace
Go to Settings → Partner / Shared Account → "Create Workspace". A unique 8-character invite code is generated.
2
Share the invite code
Tap "Copy Invite Code" and send it to your business partner via WhatsApp or any message.
3
Partner joins
The partner opens the app, goes to Settings → Partner Account → "Join with Code" → enters the 8-character code.
4
Both can now see all data
Every entry, stock item, and customer is now shared. All edits show a label indicating who made the change.
Both partners can create invoices and reports using all shared entries — perfect for businesses with two owners.
📵Installing the App & Using Offline
1
Open in the right browser
Use Chrome on Android or Safari on iPhone. Other browsers may not support installation.
2
Android: Install the app
Tap the 3-dot menu (⋮) at the top right → tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App" → tap Install. The app icon appears on your home screen.
3
iPhone: Add to Home Screen
Tap the Share button (box with arrow) at the bottom → scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap Add in the top right.
4
Use it anywhere, even without internet
The app works fully offline. Add entries, check balances, manage stock — all with no internet. When you reconnect, everything syncs automatically.
You can also install on your laptop or desktop computer. In Chrome, look for the install icon (⊕) in the address bar.

About

Why we built
Hisab-Kitab.

Hisab-Kitab

Nepal has over 800,000 small businesses — from tea shops in Kathmandu to hardware stores in Pokhara to Nepali restaurants in Sydney and Melbourne. Most of them run entirely on paper, WhatsApp groups, and memory. There's no affordable, Nepali-language tool built for how they actually work.

We built Hisab-Kitab to change that. Our goal is simple: give every Nepali shopkeeper — wherever they are in the world — access to the same business tools that large companies use. Completely free, in their own language, and simple enough to learn in under a minute.

Hisab-Kitab is built with love for Nepal's entrepreneurial spirit. Every feature is designed specifically for how Nepali businesses operate — from 13% VAT compliance to WhatsApp bill sharing to the ability to track "Udharo" (credit) customers.
